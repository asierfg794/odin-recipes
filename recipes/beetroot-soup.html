Beetroot Soup (Šaltibarščiai)

Description

Šaltibarščiai is a traditional Lithuanian cold beetroot soup, perfect for hot summer days. This vibrant pink soup is refreshing, nutritious, and bursting with flavor. It's typically served with boiled potatoes and hard-boiled eggs, making it a complete and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 liter beetroot juice (or liquid from pickled beetroots)
  • 500ml kefir or buttermilk
  • 200ml sour cream
  • 4 hard-boiled eggs
  • 4 medium potatoes
  • 1 large cucumber, diced
  • 4-5 radishes, sliced
  • 3-4 spring onions, chopped
  • Fresh dill, chopped
  • 2 pickled beetroots, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes with skin until tender, then cool and peel
  2. Hard-boil the eggs, cool in cold water, then peel
  3. In a large bowl, combine beetroot juice with kefir and sour cream
  4. Add lemon juice and mix well
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  6. Add diced cucumber, sliced radishes, and chopped spring onions
  7. Stir in the grated pickled beetroots
  8. Chill the soup in refrigerator for at least 2 hours
  9. Serve cold with boiled potatoes and halved hard-boiled eggs
  10. Garnish with fresh dill before serving
