Paella Valenciana

Description

Spanish Paella is one of the most iconic dishes of Spanish cuisine. Originally from Valencia, this delicious recipe combines rice with chicken, rabbit, green beans, and lima beans, all cooked with saffron that gives it its characteristic golden color.

Ingredients

  • 400g bomba rice (or short-grain rice)
  • 1 chicken, cut into pieces
  • 300g rabbit, cut into pieces
  • 200g green beans
  • 150g lima beans
  • 2 grated tomatoes
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 liter chicken broth
  • Saffron threads
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lemon for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in the paella pan and sauté chicken and rabbit until golden brown
  2. Add green beans and lima beans, sauté for 5 minutes
  3. Add grated tomato and red pepper, cook for 3 minutes
  4. Add rice and mix well with all ingredients
  5. Pour hot broth and add saffron threads
  6. Cook on high heat for 10 minutes, then reduce heat
  7. Continue cooking for 15-20 more minutes without stirring
  8. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving
  9. Garnish with lemon slices
